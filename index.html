Who

I am

My name is Plamen and I’m a digital designer with a great interest in Front-end. I’m a curious person by nature and I like to challenge myself so I can expand my knowledge and skills. For me visual language is very important and the right choice of shapes and colors is essential for sending the right message to the right audience. That I achieve by using my knowledge within design and UI, and keeping consistency across the different platforms. My desire for working on User Interfaces makes me always seek for improvement of my skills, so they are up to date. In this way, I can be a helpful team player and by having a better understanding of the technical side of projects, I can be a part of delivering quality end products. That comes from my background in Web Development, as well as Multimedia Design and Communication. Knowing the processes I’m able to have better communication with the Dev team.
